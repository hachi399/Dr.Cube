Getting Started

This application is designed to help you manage your tasks efficiently. Follow the steps below to get started:

  1. Create an account or log in if you already have one.
  2. Set up your profile and preferences.
  3. Add your tasks and organize them into categories.
  4. Track your progress and complete tasks.

Features

  • Task Management
  • Progress Tracking
  • Customizable Notifications
  • Collaboration Tools
